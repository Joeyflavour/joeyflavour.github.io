WHO'S BEHIND THE BEATS

About Joey Flavour

Yo, I'm Joey — 24 years old, based in The Netherlands. I've been making music for almost a decade now, and it's been a wild ride. I specialize in electronic music production and beatmaking, working across genres from future bass to melodic techno to straight-up club bangers.

My philosophy is simple: make music that you feel in your chest. I'm all about collaboration and connecting with other creatives — that's why I put my demos out there for the world to hear. If you vibe with what I do, let's create something special together.
